Frequently Asked Questions

What services are offered at the student center?

The student center provides access to the study rooms, event halls, lounge areas, and tech equipped meeting spaces.

How can I book a room?

You can book a room by using the form on the Book a Room page. Fill out the desired information, choose a room and submit your request.

Is there a limit on how long I can book a room for?

Yes, room reservations are limited to a maximum of 3 hours.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can by contacting the front desk or emailing studentcenter@uh.edu at least 24 hours in advance.

Are walk-ins available?

Walk-in bookings are available if the room is NOT reserved. Check with the front desk for availability.
